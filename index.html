In Japan because of downpours decided to evacuate the whole city.



Evacuation is announced in Japan for all 74 thousand inhabitants of Inuyama city in Aichi Prefecture because of heavy rainfall, Kyodo agency reported on Friday. In the area of ​​the city there are torrential rains, only in an hour it fell to 120 millimeters of precipitation, which creates a threat of landslides. Last week, torrential rains led to numerous floods and landslides on the south-western island of Kyushu. As a result of the disaster, 30 people were killed, more than ten are still missing.

Scientists from Oxford and Harvard Universities (Great Britain) have determined what kind of living organisms can exist on the Earth literally before the "apocalypse". The hardiest to admit was the quiet.
